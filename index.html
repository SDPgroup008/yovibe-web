YoVibe Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 27, 2026

Thank you for using YoVibe. This Privacy Policy explains how YoVibe ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects the information of users of our mobile application (the "App"). This policy applies to information collected through the App and related services.

1. Summary

  • What we collect: Location (precise), profile & contact details you provide (email, name), photos and media you upload, device and diagnostic data, and analytics/usage data.
  • Why we collect it: To provide core app features (maps, venue discovery, directions), enable social features (uploading photos), send notifications, and improve the App.
  • Who we share with: Firebase (Firestore, Storage, Authentication), Google Maps Platform (Geocoding, Places, Directions), Google Analytics/Crashlytics, and other third‑party service providers as described below.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

Account information: If you sign up or log in, we may collect your email address, display name, and profile photo. Content you create or upload: images, comments, event details, and any other content you choose to add to venues or events.

2.2 Location Information

Precise location: To show nearby venues and provide directions we may collect your device’s precise location while you use map features. Location is only used when you grant permission.

2.3 Device and Usage Information

Device identifiers, OS version, app version, IP address, and basic device characteristics. Usage and analytics: which screens you visit, features used, crash reports, and performance data.

2.4 Automatically Collected Data

Logs and diagnostics to help troubleshoot problems and improve reliability.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use information to provide and maintain App features (maps, venue listings, directions); store and serve uploaded images and content; personalize content and recommendations; send push notifications; detect, prevent, and address technical issues; and analyze and improve the App.

4. Sharing and Third Parties

We may share information with the following categories of third parties: service providers (Firebase, Google Maps Platform), and in response to legal requests. Third parties operate under their own privacy policies such as Firebase: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

5. Data Retention

We retain data as long as necessary to provide the App and for legitimate business purposes, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. You can request deletion of your account and associated data; see Contact below.

6. Your Choices and Controls

  • Location permissions: You can choose to grant or deny location access in your device settings.
  • Account and content: You can edit or delete content you uploaded from within the App (if available) or request deletion by contacting us.
  • Notifications: You may opt out of push notifications in the App or via device settings.

7. Data Security

We use commercially reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect personal information. No service is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children

The App is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, contact us to request deletion.
