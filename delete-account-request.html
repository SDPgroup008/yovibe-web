YoVibe — Account Deletion Request

This page explains how to request permanent deletion of your YoVibe account and personal data. You can host this page on a public HTTPS URL and reference it in the Google Play Console as the "Account deletion" contact page.

Quick in-app option

If you can open the YoVibe app: go to Settings > Account > Delete Account and follow the prompts. This deletes your account and associated data after verification.

Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app, email us at reinolmartin0001@gmail.com with the subject line:

YoVibe Account Deletion Request

Please include the following information in the message to speed processing:

  • The email address associated with your YoVibe account
  • Your YoVibe user ID (if known)
  • A short confirmation that you consent to permanent deletion

What we will delete

When we process your deletion request we will delete:

  • Your YoVibe account (authentication record)
  • Your public profile information (display name, bio, profile photo)
  • Content you created (posts, venues, events, vibe images) — where possible we remove media from storage
  • User-specific metadata stored in Firestore linked to your account

What may be retained

Certain data may be retained in limited form for legitimate business, legal, or security reasons:

  • Backups and logs may be retained for up to 30 days to support recovery and abuse investigations.
  • Aggregate or anonymized analytics data that cannot be tied back to an identifiable user may be retained indefinitely.
  • Records required for legal compliance (e.g., tax or fraud investigations) may be retained as required by law.

Processing time

We will acknowledge your request within 5 business days and complete deletion within 30 calendar days where possible. If additional verification is required we will contact you at the email you provide.

Questions or disputes

If you have questions about the deletion process or need to dispute the outcome, email reinolmartin0001@gmail.com.
